Features
November 16, 2016

This Online Inventory Management Software consists of several key components, all working together to create a cohesive inventory for organization's system. This features are:

  • Order Management

    • Should inventory reach a specific threshold? A company's inventory management system can be programmed to tell managers to reorder a certain product needed. This helps companies avoid running out of products or stocks or trying to put up too much capital in their inventory.

  • Service Management

    • Companies that are primarily service-oriented rather than product-oriented can use the inventory management software to track the cost of the materials they use to provide services, such as cleaning supplies. This way, they can attach prices to their services that reflect the total cost of performing them.

  • Asset Tracking

    • When a product is in warehouse or store, it can be tracked via its barcode and/or other tracking criteria, such as serial number, lot number or revision number.

  • Inventory Optimization

    • A fully automated demand forecasting and inventory optimization system to attain key inventory optimization metrics such as:

    • Reorder Point: the number of units that should trigger a replinishment order.
    • Order Quantity: the number of units that should be reordered, based on the reorder point, stock on hand, and stock on order.
    • Lead Demand: the number of units that will be sold during the lead time.
    • Stock cover: the number of days left before a stockout if no reorder is made.
    • Accuracy: the expected accuracy of the forecast.
